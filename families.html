If your loved one is detained or disappeared

Start here. Stay calm. Act quickly. You are not alone.

🚨 Emergency Checklist

  • 📱 Record the name, time, and place of detention
  • 📸 Take screenshots of any communication or notices
  • 📞 Contact a legal aid group or civil rights organization
  • 📝 Write down who told you what — names and agencies
  • 🧾 Save all documents, messages, and receipts

🗣️ What to Say to Authorities

  • Ask: “Are you holding my family member? Under what authority?”
  • Do not guess. Do not lie. Ask clearly and calmly.
  • Say: “I would like to speak to a lawyer.”
  • Do not sign anything under pressure.
