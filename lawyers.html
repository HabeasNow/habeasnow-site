Calling All Attorneys

Help us defend liberty in real time. This toolkit connects families in crisis with the legal system—fast.

🧰 What This Site Offers

  • 📄 Auto-generated habeas petitions and TROs
  • ⚖️ Jurisdictional mapping to local courts
  • 📥 PDF and email-ready output
  • 🔐 No data retention — privacy by default

📬 How Lawyers Can Help

  • Contribute improved legal templates
  • Volunteer as a point-of-contact in your jurisdiction
  • Review submitted filings before they are sent
  • Help develop pro bono response protocols

🧭 Get Involved

Email legal@habeasnow.org to join the pro bono network, submit templates, or request to collaborate. We welcome practitioners from all fields—immigration, civil rights, public defense, and beyond.

🧼 Ethics and Privacy

We store no data, track no visitors, and expect participating lawyers to comply with all applicable rules of professional responsibility. This is a tool—not legal representation.
