How This Works

From panic to paperwork — this toolkit guides you through the emergency filing process, step by step.

⚙️ What the App Does

  • 📝 Collects key info via a step-by-step form
  • 📍 Maps jurisdiction based on location and facts
  • 📄 Generates pre-filled legal documents (editable)
  • 📤 Lets users download, print, or email their filings
  • 🔐 Stores nothing. Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it.

📌 Designed for Families and Lawyers

Whether you're in crisis or standing by to help, this toolkit gives you a fast, private way to push back against unlawful detention and extrajudicial transfer. It's not legal advice. It's legal action, simplified.

🧭 What Gets Generated

  1. Emergency Habeas Petition
    A complete filing under 28 U.S.C. § 2241, asserting violations of constitutional rights and requesting immediate judicial review.
  2. Declaration and Signature Page
    A sworn statement from the filer, submitted under penalty of perjury.
  3. Temporary Restraining Order (TRO)
    If the risk of disappearance or removal is flagged, a TRO draft will be included asking the court to stop any transfer.
  4. Service Instructions
    Addresses and guidance for notifying the Department of Justice, Attorney General, and U.S. Attorney.
  5. Filing Court Locator (Beta)
    Enter a ZIP code to determine the U.S. District Court with jurisdiction.
